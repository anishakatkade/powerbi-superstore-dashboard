# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard - Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales performance, profit trends, regional insights, and top-performing products.  
The dashboard helps in business decision-making through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🚀 Key Features
- KPI Cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, and Total Quantity
- Sales by Category Analysis
- Profit Analysis by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend Visualization
- Top 5 Products by Sales
- Regional Sales Distribution
- Interactive Slicers for Category and Region Filtering
- Business Insights for better decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence

---

## 📈 Business Insights
- Technology category generated the highest sales
- West region achieved maximum profit
- Top-performing products contributed major revenue
- Monthly sales trends helped identify business patterns
